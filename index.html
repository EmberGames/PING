PING

Alt-Rock/Indie Rock

The Project

PING is an indie rock/ alt-rock band focusing on quality music. We use instruments such as Piano, Drums, and Guitar in our songs.

Built entirely from the ground up, starting in a small town in Oklahoma with 3 kids, PING has ambitious dreams.

Current Status

New material is currently being made.

PING's first album is in the makings, with the team making amazing, and speedy progress with it. For More Info, Teasers, Release Dates, and more, will be posted on certain social media sites.

Who We Are

​Carter – Founder, primary songwriter, lead vocalist, and pianist. He serves as the creative director and foundational architect behind PING's melodic structure.
Joel – Lead guitarist and visual reviewer. A day-one pillar of the group, he shapes the atmospheric string textures and visual identity of the project.
Odin – Lead drummer and production coordinator. He drives the core rhythmic pulse of every track while systematically organizing the project's timelines and tracking baselines.
