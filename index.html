The Project

PING is an indie rock/ alt-rock band focusing on quality music. We use instruments such as Piano, Drums, and Guitar in our songs.

Built entirely from the ground up, starting in a small town in Oklahoma with 3 kids, PING has ambitious dreams.

Current Status

New material is currently being made.

PING's first album is in the makings, with the team making amazing, and speedy progress with it. For More Info, Teasers, Release Dates, and more, will be posted on certain social media sites.
